Tray
PopTrayU will display an indicator in your system tray at all times.



This tray icon is useful to see at a glance how many new messages are waiting for you.
There are no new messages for you.
There is 1 new message for you. The red circle indicates it is in the account that you marked as Red.
Currently busy connecting to a POP3 server and checking for new mail.
Currently busy deleting messages from the server
There was an error connecting to one of your accounts. The status bar of that account will show the error message.
AutoCheck is disabled. PopTrayU will not automatically check for new mail.

Clicking on the Tray Icon can be customized on the Options tab, to perform any of the predefined actions.
